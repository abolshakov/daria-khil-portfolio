Hello!

I am a constantly-drawing artist, who is very passionate about her work and loves creating new worlds, characters and stories about them.

For me, the most exciting thing about art is an ability to tell any idea and story in an accessible and beautiful way in order to bring it to people.

I have been studying art since I was 10 and working in this field for 6 years. During this time I've participated in a couple of game projects as a concept artist and illustrator, created animated videos, illustrated some published books, websites and other interesting stuff.

In my spare time, I love looking for inspiration in animated movies, cartoons, video games and nature.

I'm looking forward to joining new creative teams and exciting projects! So, feel free to contact me for any work inquiries or just to say hi at hundred.dots@gmail.com
